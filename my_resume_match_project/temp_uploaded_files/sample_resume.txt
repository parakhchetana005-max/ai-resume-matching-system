AI Engineer

and optimising image generation pipelines using Stable Diffusion and enhancing performance with
Low-Rank Adaptations (LORAs). The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience with AI models
and tools like ComfyUI, vector databases, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) frameworks.
Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and optimize Stable Diffusion pipelines for generating high-quality images.
- Build and fine-tune LORAs to enhance model performance for specific use cases.
- Design and implement custom image processing workflows using ComfyUI.
- Integrate and manage vector databases to support RAG for enhanced data retrieval and model
efficiency.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver AI solutions tailored to business needs.
- Continuously improve AI models and processes to enhance performance, scalability, and reliability.
Qualifications:
- Proven experience with Stable Diffusion and ComfyUI.
- Strong understanding of LORAs and their practical applications in AI model optimization.
- Hands-on experience with vector databases and implementing RAG frameworks.
- Proficiency in Python and AI/ML libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or others.
- Ability to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment, demonstrating strong problem-
solving
skills and attention to detail.

- Familiarity with MLOps practices, including deploying and maintaining AI models in production.- Knowledge of other AI frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch).
